NACHC 2022 HIV CDS Connectathon: Information and Resources

Introduction

This page contains all of the resources required for the 2022 NACHC HIV-CDS Connectathon.

To fully participate in the connectathon, participants should have Postman installed on a computer they can use during the connectathon. Participants should be able to access the following URLs from the computer they will be using during the connectathon.

The SmartHealthIt SMART on FHIR Sandbox:
https://launch.smarthealthit.org
The SmartHealthIt SMART on FHIR R4 Patient FHIR server:
https://launch.smarthealthit.org/v/r4/fhir/Patient
